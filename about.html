Welcome to Lumin

Our wallet API is designed to simplify and enhance payment system integration for developers, merchants, and end-users alike, with a focus on speed, security, and flexibility. We offer a robust set of features, including cross-chain compatibility, multi-currency support, customizable transaction fees, and real-time transaction monitoring, all backed by top-notch encryption and anti-fraud measures to ensure the highest level of data privacy and security.


Our dedicated team of experts is committed to providing reliable, round-the-clock support to our clients, and constantly improving our API with the latest technology and industry standards to meet the evolving needs of the market.
